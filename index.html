About The Company

Andrey Butnaru specializes in flooring and remodeling homes. He began his business, Floortastic Flooring, in 2018 with the support of his wife and children. He started installing and replacing flooring/ tiling. Over the past 4 years, the services he provides have grown to a wide variety of remodeling and restoration services.

He specializes in providing high-quality services. His customers are pleased with the communication throughout the whole project. He will be there with you every step of the way.
